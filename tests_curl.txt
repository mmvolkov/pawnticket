Регистрация нового пользователя

curl.exe --location 'http://localhost:8080/api/auth/signup?username=testuser&email=test%40example.com&password=password123&firstName=Test&lastName=User' \
--header 'Content-Type: application/json' \
--data-raw '{
    "username": "zuev",
    "email": "zuev@example.com",
    "password": "password123",
    "firstName": "Ivan",
    "lastName": "Zuev"
}'


Вход в систему (получение токена)

curl.exe --location "http://localhost:8080/api/auth/login" --header "Content-Type: application/json" --data "{"username": "petrov","password": "password123"}"
